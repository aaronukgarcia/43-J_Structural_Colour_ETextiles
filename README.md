## Overview

The most technically complex proposal in Project 43. Proposes integrating three independently unproven technologies in a single fibre: permanent structural colour via B&oslash;ggild-architecture polymer coatings, electronic conductivity via CNT/conductive polymer yarn, and piezoelectric energy harvesting. Integration risk is multiplicative and likely worse due to correlated failure modes.

## Key Idea

Textile dyeing produces 20% of global industrial water pollution. Structural colour eliminates dye-related water pollution from the colouring step. Combining dye-free colour with conductivity and energy harvesting in a single fibre could create a convergent textile platform â€” but three independent breakthroughs are required.
